About Me

Crafting Trustworthy AI for a Better World

I'm a Software Engineering undergraduate majoring in Data Science at Daffodil International University, with a near-perfect CGPA of 3.995/4.00. My passion lies in making AI that is not just accurate — but interpretable, reliable, and socially responsible.

My current thesis, TruBrain-XAI, focuses on multimodal deep learning for brain tumor classification, integrating uncertainty quantification and explainable AI to support transparent clinical decision-making.

I believe the most important AI breakthroughs are tools that give people the clarity to act with confidence and dignity.
