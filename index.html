🧠Multimodal MRI Fusion

Integrates four MRI sequences — T1, T1-contrast, T2, and FLAIR — alongside structured clinical metadata. Feature-level and decision-level fusion strategies are evaluated to determine which combination yields the richest diagnostic signal.

T1 · T1c · T2 · FLAIR · Clinical Metadata
🔍Explainable AI Integration

Deploys Grad-CAM for spatial attention heatmaps, SHAP for feature-level attribution, and counterfactual reasoning to answer "what-if" clinical questions. Every prediction is paired with visual and quantitative explanations.

Grad-CAM · SHAP · Counterfactuals
📊Uncertainty Quantification

Incorporates Bayesian deep learning and Monte Carlo Dropout to estimate model confidence per prediction. Low-confidence cases trigger human review — a critical safety mechanism for clinical deployment.

Bayesian DL · MC Dropout · Calibration
🏥Clinical Trustworthiness

Beyond performance metrics, this research rigorously evaluates whether explanations highlight diagnostically meaningful regions and whether predictions remain calibrated under data shift — the transparency bar required for real-world healthcare.

Calibration · Robustness · Clinical Safety
