About Me

I'm a Software Engineering undergraduate majoring in Data Science at Daffodil International University, holding a near-perfect CGPA of 3.995 / 4.00. My passion lies in building AI that isn't just accurate — but interpretable, fair, and clinically deployable.

My current undergraduate thesis focuses on Trustworthy Multimodal XAI for brain tumor classification, merging multi-sequence MRI analysis with Grad-CAM and SHAP to build diagnostic AI that clinicians can genuinely trust.

I believe the most impactful AI breakthroughs are those built with human dignity and transparency at their core — not just optimized loss functions.
